Medical Incident Transcriber

M.I.T School Back-End Capstone

M.I.T is a mobile application thats allows first responders the ability to transcribe the audio during an emergency incident. The first responder can then look at the time stamped records and see exactly when certain events happened. This is especially crucial information when pushing drugs or starting CPR. The User can also upload images to the app for reference (I.E a picture of the car damage after a bad wreck). Users can take notes and put in other relevant information that would help them write their report or if they have to go to court they will have the details of what happened. Built the front-end with REACT.JS and the server-side with Sql server express and ASP.Net.
